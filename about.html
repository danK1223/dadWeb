BIOGRAPHY

Ronen Kaufman was born in Tel Aviv on November 1st 1966. Served in the Israeli Navy for 3 years. studied Engineering at the Tel Aviv University School of Engineering. Active Member of Israel packaging design institute. Kaufman’s art studies began in Tel Aviv in 1990 in the Mirof Art Institute, Tel Aviv. The move towards art came as a result of the tragic death of his brother Ilan in an airforce helicopter accident. Ilan was at that time an architectural student at the Technion in Haifa, and a reserve pilot in the Israel Air Force. The first colors used by Kaufman were those which he came across in his brother’s apartment following the accident. The artistic career of Ronen Kaufman started 1990 after the tragic death of his brother in a helicopter accident. The first colors which were used by Kaufman were those which remained in his brothers apartment who at that time studied architecture in the Technion in Haifa. Kaufman’s art studies started in Tel Aviv as one of the students of Jacob Mishori and continued in the studio in Raanana and activity in Tel Aviv Museum Art Centre.
Kaufman comments: “In my work I am in a continuous search for the real people and real life which I am trying to portray in a visual way with colors on the canvas.” “The creation is for me a symbol of existence and self realization.” “The actual work is a process of continuation of creativity. “ The works are expressing mostly the vitality of life with all its aspects.
