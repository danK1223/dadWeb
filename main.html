Ronen Kaufman was born in Tel Aviv on November 1st 1966. Active Member of Israel packaging design institute. Kaufman’s art studies began in Tel Aviv in 1990 in the Mirof Art Institute, Tel Aviv. The move towards art came as a result of the tragic death of his brother Ilan in an airforce helicopter accident.
